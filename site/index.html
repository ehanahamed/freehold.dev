A studying tool that's actually free

Quizfreely is a free and open source learning app with flashcards, review/learn mode, practice tests, & more!

Get Started Explore

It's actually free

Quizfreely has unlimited practice tests for free, unlike other flashcard websites or studying apps. Quizfreely is open source and nonprofit, and there are no paid features or subscriptions.

Advanced features to measure & improve your studying habits

Quizfreely records individual terms' & flashcards' progress & accuracy. It calculates frequently confused terms or definitions and finds which incorrect answer choices are selected the most. Also, there's cool graphs and charts and stuff.

Quizfreely is nonprofit

Quizfreely is a nonprofit project with transparent finances. We're fiscally sponsored by The Hack Foundation, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit.
